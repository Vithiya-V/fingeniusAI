# FinGenius AI – Financial Intelligence Platform
FinGenius AI is an AI-powered financial intelligence platform built with Streamlit, Python, Gemini API, and Yahoo Finance. It helps beginners analyze stocks, compare companies, evaluate portfolios, upload financial datasets, and gain AI-driven investment insights through interactive visualizations and simplified financial guidance.
## Features
### Stock Analyzer
* Analyze publicly traded companies using stock ticker symbols.
* View company fundamentals such as:
  * Market Capitalization
  * PE Ratio
  * EPS
  * Revenue Growth
  * Debt-to-Equity Ratio
* Interactive stock performance charts.
* AI-generated investment analysis and recommendations.
### Stock Comparison
* Compare two companies side-by-side.
* Analyze financial metrics and growth potential.
* AI-generated comparative reports.
### Portfolio Analyzer
* Analyze investment portfolios.
* Diversification assessment.
* Risk evaluation and portfolio health insights.
* Asset allocation visualization.
### Dataset Analyzer
* Upload CSV or Excel financial datasets.
* Automated statistical analysis.
* Trend visualization through charts.
* AI-powered financial interpretation.
### Dataset Comparison
* Compare multiple company datasets.
* Identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and risks.
* Generate comparative financial intelligence reports.
### AI Financial Tutor
* Interactive AI assistant for financial education.
* Explains financial concepts in beginner-friendly language.
* Provides investment-related guidance and learning support.
### AI-Powered Insights
* Powered by Google Gemini API.
* Generates:
  * Executive summaries
  * Financial health assessments
  * Risk analysis
  * Growth potential analysis
  * Investor suitability recommendations
  * SWOT analysis
  * Investment decision flowcharts
## Technologies Used
* Python
* Streamlit
* Google Gemini API
* Yahoo Finance (yFinance)
* Pandas
* Plotly
* OpenPyXL
* Python Dotenv
## Project Objectives
The primary objective of FinGenius AI is to simplify financial decision-making for beginner investors by transforming complex financial information into actionable and easy-to-understand insights using Artificial Intelligence.

FinGenius AI is developed for educational and research purposes. The investment insights generated by the platform should not be considered financial advice. Users should conduct their own research before making investment decisions.
